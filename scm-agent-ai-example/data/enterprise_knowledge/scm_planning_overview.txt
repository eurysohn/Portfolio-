SCM Planning Overview

Demand planning aligns statistical forecasts with commercial inputs, promotions, and new product introductions. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

The S&OP cycle balances demand, supply, inventory, and financial targets with a monthly executive review. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Master Production Scheduling translates the demand plan into time-phased build quantities and available-to-promise logic. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

MRP explodes the BOM to calculate gross requirements, net requirements, and planned order releases. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Common planning KPIs include forecast accuracy, bias, and adherence to schedule. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
