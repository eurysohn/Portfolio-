SCM Analytics and KPIs

Fill rate measures the percent of customer demand fulfilled immediately from stock. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

OTIF measures the percent of orders delivered on time and in full. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Forecast accuracy is often tracked with MAPE or WMAPE. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Inventory accuracy compares system on-hand to physical count. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Cash-to-cash cycle time links inventory, receivables, and payables. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
