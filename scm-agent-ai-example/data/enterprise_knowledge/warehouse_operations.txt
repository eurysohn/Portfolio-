Warehouse Operations

WMS orchestrates inbound receiving, putaway, replenishment, and outbound picking. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Slotting assigns fast movers to ergonomic locations to reduce travel time. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Dock-to-stock time measures how quickly received goods are available for order fulfillment. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Cycle time and labor productivity are monitored to sustain service levels. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Quality checks reduce scrap and rework by catching issues at receiving. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
