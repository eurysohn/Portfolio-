Supplier Management

Supplier lead time and on-time delivery are central to supply continuity. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

MOQ and lot size constraints affect inventory investment and schedule flexibility. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Supplier scorecards include quality, responsiveness, and cost competitiveness. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Dual sourcing reduces risk from single-supplier disruption. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Collaborative planning with suppliers stabilizes production schedules. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
