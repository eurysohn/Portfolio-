Planning Exceptions

Expedite orders when customer priorities override standard lead time. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Past-due orders require root-cause analysis and corrective action. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Shortage reports highlight components that constrain production schedules. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Allocation rules define fair distribution during supply shortages. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Demand sensing uses near-real-time data to adjust forecasts. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
