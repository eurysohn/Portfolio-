Inventory Optimization

EOQ balances ordering costs with holding costs to determine optimal batch size. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Safety stock can be set by service level targets using a z-score approach. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Pipeline inventory covers demand during replenishment lead time. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
