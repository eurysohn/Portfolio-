Inventory Policy Playbook

Safety stock protects service level against demand variability and lead time variation. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Reorder point is calculated as expected demand during lead time plus safety stock. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Min-max policies maintain inventory between a minimum and maximum, often for C-class items. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Cycle counting improves accuracy and reduces shrinkage by rotating counts by ABC classification. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.

Inventory turnover and days of inventory on hand measure capital efficiency. This guidance is reviewed quarterly to reflect changing business conditions.
